3D printing material (ABS)

ABS or Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene is a thermoplastic polymer that is strong, durable, and heat-resistant. It is commonly used in 3D printing because it can be melted and molded repeatedly without degrading its quality. The Netra Fundus Camera is made of ABS, which ensures its durability and reliability.

3D printing technology (FDM)

FDM or Fused Deposition Modeling is a type of 3D printing technology that works by melting a thermoplastic filament and depositing it layer by layer to form a 3D object. It is a cost-effective and efficient way of producing complex and customized parts, such as the Netra Fundus Camera.

Assembly method (by hand in store rooms)

The Netra Fundus Camera is assembled by hand in store rooms. This means that each unit is carefully crafted and inspected by a skilled technician, ensuring that it meets the highest standards of quality and performance. Additionally, this method allows for greater flexibility in the manufacturing process, making it easier to customize the product to meet the needs of different customers.
